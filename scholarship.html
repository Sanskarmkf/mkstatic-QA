About The Program

About Our Scholarship Program

At Sanskar Foundation, we firmly believe that financial constraints should never stand in the way of a student's potential. Our scholarship program is designed to identify and support talented young individuals who have the drive to succeed but lack the resources.

Through this initiative, we aim to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity — helping students focus on their education without the burden of financial stress.

  • Open to students from economically weaker sections
  • Merit-based selection process
  • Support for academic fees and educational materials
  • Mentorship and guidance provided alongside financial aid

Sanskar Merit Scholarship

Supporting deserving students in pursuing their academic dreams

Now Open

Eligibility

  • Students from Class 8 to Graduation level
  • Family annual income below ₹2,50,000
  • Minimum 60% marks in previous year
  • Strong will to continue education

What We Cover

  • School / College tuition fees
  • Books and stationery
  • Uniform and basic supplies
  • Career guidance sessions

How to Apply

Fill out our application form with your academic records and family income proof. Our team will review and reach out within 2 weeks of submission.

Required Documents

  • Previous year marksheet
  • Income certificate
  • Aadhar card copy
  • Passport size photograph
