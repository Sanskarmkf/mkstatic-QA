About Our Scholarship Program

Sanskar MKFoundation is committed to supporting the education of underprivileged children by providing financial assistance through scholarships. Our program covers tuition fees and other educational expenses for deserving students, ensuring they receive a quality education without financial burden.

Through this initiative, we aim to reduce school dropout rates, empower students for a better future, and encourage education among those from economically weaker sections of society.

  • Family income below the poverty line (BPL)
  • Minimum 50% marks in previous examination
  • At least 75% school attendance required
  • Special preference for orphans, single-parent & differently-abled students

Eligibility Criteria

  • Annual family income below poverty line (BPL)
  • Enrolled in a recognized school or institution
  • Minimum 50% marks in last examination
  • Minimum 75% school attendance
  • Special preference: orphans, single-parent & differently-abled

What We Cover

  • School / institution tuition fees
  • Books and stationery
  • Other educational expenses
  • Regular monitoring and follow-up support

Application Process

  • Fill out & submit the application form with documents
  • Screening & document verification by our team
  • Home visits may be conducted for verification
  • Selected students receive approval confirmation
  • Funds disbursed directly to the school/institution

Required Documents

  • Income certificate / BPL card / Ration card
  • Previous year mark sheet (min. 50%)
  • Aadhaar card & birth certificate
  • School bonafide / admission certificate
  • Passport-size photographs (2–4 copies)
  • Recommendation letter from school principal
  • Bank passbook copy (student / guardian)
