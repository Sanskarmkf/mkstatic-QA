About Sanskar

Sanskar is a student-led charitable group driven by compassionate individuals dedicated to helping those in need. We provide essential support such as food, clothing, education, books, and medicine. Our mission is to promote equality, ease the burdens of those facing urgent hardships, and uplift honest workers with dignity and fairness.
The foundation day of Sanskar is January 26, 2023.
