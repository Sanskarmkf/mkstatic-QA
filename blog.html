FROM SWEAT TO SUCCESS | RECOGNIZING THE INVISIBLE WORKFORCE ON LABOUR’S DAY

May. 1st

Labour Day is a moment to honor the dedication, hard work, and efforts of workers who keep institutions running smoothly. We all see clean hallways, neatly trimmed shrubs, clear windows, and beautifully kept gardens in our colleges…Who are these unrecognized caretakers of our institutions who make this happen? On May 1st, 2024, the Sanskar Foundation took the initiative to celebrate and appreciate the invaluable contribution of non-teaching staff at Acharya Institutes. The event, held at Acharya Institutes, Bengaluru, was a small effort to acknowledge and express gratitude to these essential members of this community.
