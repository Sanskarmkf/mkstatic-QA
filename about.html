Introduction

The Foundation Day of the sanskar is Jan 26,2023. Sanskar is a charitable group driven by compassionate individuals aiming to aid those in need. We extend a helping hand with vital necessities such as food, clothing, education, books, and medicine, fostering equality and to ease the burdens of those facing urgent hardships, promoting fairness and supporting honest workers along the way.

Programs & Activities

  • • Education Empowerment
  • • Mental Support, Financial Support, Psychological Support, Career Growth
  • • Women Empowerment
  • • Old Age Home Visit
  • • Orphanage Visit
  • • Animal Welfare

Our Goals

  • • Empower communities to make the world a peaceful place.
  • • Ensure everyone has access to essentials, opportunities for growth, smiles, happiness, and quality education worldwide.
