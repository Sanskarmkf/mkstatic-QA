Moments of Happiness

Date: February 18, 2023

On February 18, 2023, the Sanskar Foundation spent a delightful day at Sri Saibaba Old Age & Orphan Children’s Home in Bengaluru, bringing joy and comfort to the children. The team handed out snacks, shared meaningful conversations, and created joyful moments to make every child feel cherished and appreciated.. The visit was a day filled with smiles, laughter, and positivity, leaving a lasting impact on the children and reinforcing the importance of compassion in transforming lives.

More than just a charitable act, this visit symbolized the Sanskar Foundation’s dedication to uplifting vulnerable communities. Spending time with the children offered valuable insights into their challenges and highlighted the importance of emotional connection alongside material support. Such initiatives remind us that small gestures of kindness can inspire hope and happiness, encouraging the foundation to continue its mission of creating a brighter, more inclusive future for every child
