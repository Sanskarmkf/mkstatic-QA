Haldwani Event

On November 20, 2023, we supported over 70 underprivileged students in Haldwani with educational supplies and treats. This initiative aims to inspire and equip young minds for a brighter future. The smiles of the children made the day unforgettable.

Children Welfare

On February 18, 2023, we spent a joyful day at Sri Saibaba Old Age & Orphan Children’s Home in Bengaluru. We distributed snacks and created happy memories, ensuring every child felt loved. This visit reinforced our commitment to uplifting vulnerable communities.

Labour Recognition

On May 1, 2024, we celebrated Labor Day by appreciating the staff at Acharya Institute of Technology. We distributed fruits and essentials as a gesture of gratitude for their hard work. This act highlighted the importance of recognizing those who support our community.

Bringing Smiles to Young Hearts

We are deeply grateful to the Breeze Foundation for enabling us to organize A Visit to the Serve Needy Orphanage, Bengaluru, where we aimed to instill social values, provide career guidance, and create awareness about drug usage and environmental cleanliness.
